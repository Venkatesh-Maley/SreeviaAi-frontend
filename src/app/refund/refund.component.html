Refund & Cancellation Policy

Understand our approach to refunds and cancellations to ensure transparency and trust.

1. Subscription Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Upon cancellation, you will continue to have access until the end of the billing cycle.

2. Refund Policy

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on first-time purchases. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 7 days of purchase for a full refund.

3. No Refunds After Trial

If you have used a free trial before subscribing, refunds are not applicable unless there is a billing error.

4. Billing Errors

If you believe you have been billed in error, please contact our support team within 7 days. We will review your request and process refunds where appropriate.

5. Contact Us

For any refund or cancellation inquiries, please reach out to us.
